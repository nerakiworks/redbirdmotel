Desert driving notes

Fuel, food, and late-night services can be limited along this stretch of Route 66. Plan to top off before the last long drive into Amboy.

Weather and light

Desert afternoons can run hot, while evenings cool quickly. Early morning and sunset are usually the most comfortable times for photos and short walks.

Road rhythm

Cell service may vary outside town. Download directions ahead of time and give yourself extra daylight if you plan to visit Amboy Crater.

Before you head out

Fuel and food Services can be limited and may close early. Fill up and bring snacks or water before longer desert drives.

Daylight planning Desert roads feel different after dark. If you are visiting Amboy Crater or taking photos, plan your return before sunset.

Heat and water Carry water even for short stops. Summer afternoons can be harsh, and shade is limited around the crater and open trails.
